Most of what I build comes from the same question: how do you teach a system to actually pay attention? I'm exploring that through computer vision and, more recently, through training LLMs. In my free time, I am also exploring mechatronics related projects.
</p>

---

### 🔭 Currently focused on

- 🔍 **Anomaly & defect detection** using **YOLO** and **PatchCore** for industrial/visual inspection tasks
- 🧩 **Decoding & reading codes** (barcodes/QR/data matrix) through image analysis and preprocessing pipelines
- 🖼️ Building robust **preprocessing pipelines** (denoising, thresholding, augmentation) to make CV models actually work in the wild, not just on clean datasets
- 🧠 Fine-tuning and training **open-source LLMs** for custom tasks
- 📚 Building **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems** to ground LLMs in real data
